    How Prism Works
    A three-layer decision filter, not a signal generator
    01
    Detect Market Regime
    Classifies the market as Trending, Ranging, or Volatile using EMA slope, ATR normalization, and price structure on the daily timeframe.
    02
    Identify High-Probability Zones
    Maps 4H location — pullback, breakout, or range — and detects liquidity sweeps and key structural levels with ATR-normalized precision.
    03
    Confirm or Protect Capital
    Waits for a confirmed 1H entry trigger. If conditions aren't ideal, Prism protects your capital. No forced signals. No noise.
